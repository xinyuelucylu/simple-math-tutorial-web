Conan, can you tell me what you learned today?


Yes, I learned how to convert measurements, and which measurement to use for diffrerent objects!




Summary


Kilometre (km): 1000 metres. Usually used for distance.


Hectometre (hm): 100 metres. Approximetly the size of a soccer field.


Decametre (dam): 10 metres. One practical use of the decametre is for altitude of geopotential heights in meteorology.


Metre (m): The fundamental unit of length in the metric system. It is the most commontly used measurement by everyone.


Decimetre (dm): Decimetre means one-tenth of a meter. Therefore, there are 10 decimetres in one metre.


Centimetre (cm): Usually used to mesure what people use daily.


Millimetre (mm): The smallest measurement on a ruler.
